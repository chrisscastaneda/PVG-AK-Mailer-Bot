Dear Friend,

The 2014 primary election is here! Your ballot is filled with local candidates who will make a huge impact on equality, security, and the future of Washington. Voting in the August 5 primary is an important first step toward retaking the state Senate and supporting progressives in many local races that will impact you, your neighbors and your children.

To spread the word about all the other races on your ballot, we worked with Washington's leading progressive organizations to produce the 2014 Progressive Voters Guide to the Primary -- your ticket to highly-informed voting based on progressive values.

The deadline for mailing in your ballot is August 5, but you should join your neighbors and mail it in today! Please forward this guide to your friends, family, and co-workers - they can find their own locally customized version at [link].

Thanks for all that you do, (especially voting)

Aaron and the entire team at Fuse

P.S. Only candidates that will be on the primary ballot are included in the guide.
